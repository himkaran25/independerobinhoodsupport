How to Contact Robinhood Support

Step-by-Step guide to reach official Robinhood help for your account issues.

1. Official Support Methods

📞 Call Now: +1 (888) 392-4990

🔹 In-App / Website Support: The most reliable way to contact Robinhood Support is through the Help Center inside the app or on the official website. Choose your issue and request a call or start a chat. This is available 24/7 through the support interface.

📧 Official Support Email:

  • support@robinhood.com – General support email for non-urgent questions and case follow-up.
  • reportphishing@robinhood.com – Use this address specifically to report phishing and scam attempts.

Note: Robinhood encourages you to initiate support from within your logged-in account for secure assistance whenever possible.

2. Social Media & Other Official Channels

You can follow and interact for general updates and announcements via these accounts (not a primary support channel):

  • Twitter/X: @RobinhoodApp | @AskRobinhood
  • Facebook: @RobinhoodApp
  • Instagram: @RobinhoodApp
  • LinkedIn: Robinhood company page

3. Tips Before You Contact Support

  • 📌 Always provide your registered email and a clear description of your issue.
  • 📌 Don't share passwords, 2FA codes, or sensitive personal details over social media.
  • 📌 If you receive suspicious communications claiming to be Robinhood, verify through the official support page or report them to reportphishing@robinhood.com.
